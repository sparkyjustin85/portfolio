Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

The no-frills, blue-collar Appalachia town is not glamorous, but it is definitely a special spot on the North American map. It has revitalized itself in a perfect way; former steel mill yards are now renowned art spaces and unique eateries.

Check out all the Wildlife

As the warm weather approaches, it is the perfect time to see our vast selection of exotic wildlife. With so many beautiful animals and fauna to see, it can be hard to choose where to visit. I recommend starting with one of the three best; The Pittsburgh Zoo & PPG Aquarium, The National Aviary, and Phipps Conservatory.

Lodging

Pittsburgh is full of wonderful places to stay. However, if you are feeling adventurous, just a short drive out of the city, you can enjoy two close by state parks. If the great indoors are more your style, relax like a king at one of our gorgous hotels. Here are my top recommendations:

  • Morraine State Park
  • SpringHill Suites by Marriott
  • Embassy Suites by Hilton

Food
